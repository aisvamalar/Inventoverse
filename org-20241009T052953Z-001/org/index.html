Membership Requirements

Enrolled

To participate, you must be an active student at our school. This requirement ensures that all members are engaged in the community and can benefit from the resources, activities, and opportunities provided exclusively to current students.

Innoventures

Embrace your passion for technology and innovation, exploring new ideas and advancements. Stay curious and engaged in the ever-evolving tech landscape, and seek opportunities to learn, collaborate, and contribute to groundbreaking projects that shape the future.

BiMonthly
